Welcome to Movle a clone of Wordle

Rules: The movie title is picked from TMDB api and gets English movies.
Titles are ranging in length from 5-7.
If a movie title is two words, then spaces are eliminated.
I understand some titles you might have never heard of, but that's the fun of it.
